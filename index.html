My First Blog Post

Published on:May 11,2025

Life is never a straight road. I’ve had my share of ups and downs. There was someone I truly loved—gave her my heart, accepted her family as my own, and dreamed of building a future together. But as time passed, things changed. Maybe I cared more deeply than she ever did. It hurt... it broke me... but it didn’t stop me.

What am I doing now?

Now I have one goal—to build my future. I’ve started learning front-end web development. I’ve already learned HTML, and I’m moving on to CSS. Insha'Allah, within the next 4 years, I want to land a good job, earn well, make my family proud, and marry someone who truly values and understands me.

Life will always throw challenges, but I’ve learned to turn pain into power. From now on, I’ll keep moving forward. Through this blog, I’ll share my progress, my journey—this is me, and this is my story.
